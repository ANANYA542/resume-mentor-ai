Keep your resume to one page if you are a student or fresher.
Use bullet points instead of long paragraphs.
Keep spacing and alignment consistent throughout the resume.
Highlight section titles using bold formatting.
Focus on achievements rather than responsibilities.
Tailor your resume according to the job description.
Place the most important information at the top.
Use clear and concise language.
Avoid unnecessary personal details like age or marital status.
Proofread your resume to remove grammar and spelling mistakes.
